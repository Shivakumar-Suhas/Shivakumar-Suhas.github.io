## Overview
During my internship at the **National Aerospace Laboratories (NAL), India**, I worked on **gas turbine performance analysis and high-temperature structural evaluation of turbomachinery components**.

I performed **turbojet engine analysis under both on-design and off-design conditions**, evaluating performance parameters such as thrust, efficiency, and temperature variations across the cycle. This included working with **ideal cycle models and Gasturb12**, providing insight into real engine behavior under varying operating conditions.

In parallel, I conducted a **static thermal analysis of a centrifugal impeller using ANSYS**, assessing structural integrity under **operational temperatures up to 850°C** using **SS-316L material**. The analysis focused on **thermal stress distribution, deformation, and failure margins**, ensuring safe operation under high-temperature loading.

This work provided a strong foundation in **thermal stress management, fatigue considerations, and material behavior of high-performance alloys** used in aerospace propulsion systems.
